AUTOMATIC CHARCOAL COOKING

SMOKASAURUS

Smokasaurus is hand-built in Canada by a small family-owned company, that is passionate about having amazing food for when having good times with friends and family.
Handles
The handles are made of oak wood to insulate for protecting the hands, and to add a premium feel.
Control Unit
This control unit is what sets Smokasaurus apart from other smokers on the market. Using our secure web app, you can monitor and control your smoker from anywhere via your phone, tablet or desktop.
Bottom Casing
The bottom casing is machine-engraved with the Smokasaurus logo. It holds a fan that provides the oxygen needed to fuel the combustion of the charcoal. The fan is electronically controlled via a temperature sensor so that it is always providing the right amount of circulation to maintain the perfect temperature automatically.
